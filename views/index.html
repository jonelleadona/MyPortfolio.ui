About Me

Hello! My name is Jonelle, but some call me JoJo. I am currently a student in a coding bootcamp course that is being taught in partnership with UCF. I am also a 2020 UCF graduate with a degree in health service administration. I've always had an interest in coding and web development, but I was always preoccupied with other acedmic goals throughout college. After graduating I realized that coding was something that I was really passionate about, so I decided to take the jump and pursue a career path with it. During my time as a coding student, I came to the conclusion that I made the right switch in career paths.




I spend most my days practicing and learning new logic, but aside from coding, I love to travel and hike new trails. I enjoy the outdoors as well as photographing my adventures along the way.
